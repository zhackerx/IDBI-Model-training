# MSME Credit Assessment Policy (Sample)

## ITR Verification
- Last 2 years of ITR should be available for normal approval.
- Missing ITR years require manual review.
- Tax penalties or unresolved defaults are high-risk markers.

## GST Compliance
- GST registration should be active.
- Repeated delayed GST filings indicate compliance weakness.
- Consistent turnover trend supports approval.

## Credit History
- CIBIL score below policy threshold is a risk signal.
- Written-off or settled accounts trigger stricter scrutiny.
- Multiple recent delinquencies can lead to rejection.

## Existing Loans
- High EMI burden against income increases risk.
- Debt obligations should remain within policy limits.

## Bank Statement Signals
- Frequent cheque bounces are high-risk indicators.
- Stable cash flow and healthy average balance are positive.
